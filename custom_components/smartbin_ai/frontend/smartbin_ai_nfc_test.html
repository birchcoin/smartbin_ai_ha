Troubleshooting

If you get a 404 error:

  • Ensure SmartBin AI integration is loaded in Home Assistant
  • Restart Home Assistant after making changes
  • Check that the bin ID exists (smartbin_001, smartbin_002, etc.)
  • For iOS, use the correct scheme:
    • homeassistant:// for production
    • homeassistant-beta:// for beta app
    • homeassistant-dev:// for dev app
